Scholarship Application Now Open

General Announcement
Posted on: Feb 6, 2026, 7:00 AM

We are pleased to announce that the 2024-2025 Scholarship Application is now officially open. This program is intended to support deserving students who demonstrate academic potential and financial need.

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Must be a Filipino citizen
  • Currently enrolled or accepted in an accredited college or university
  • Maintain a minimum GPA of 2.5 or equivalent
  • Come from a low-income household

Required Documents:

  • Filled-out application form
  • Certificate of Enrollment or Admission
  • Latest Report Card or Transcript of Records
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Barangay Certificate of Indigency
  • 2x2 ID Photo

Application Deadline: March 31, 2026

For inquiries, please contact us at scholarship@kkfi.org or visit our office at KKFI Building, Manila.

Target Audience: All (Scholars & Applicants)
Email Notification: Sent to 446 recipients
